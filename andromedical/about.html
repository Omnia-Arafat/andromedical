1990

The Board of Directors approved an accelerated plan to build the new Hospital in one phase, with completion expected in 1990.

2000

The executive team joined members of the medical staff and hospital leadership, board members, community dignitaries and key donors to celebrate the new hospital’s “Topping Off”, a tradition of large construction projects to commemorate the completion of major steel work.

2008

With gifts totaling $20 million from the Alexandra and Steve Cohen Foundation, the Cohen Pediatric Institute was created, which will feature a dedicated pediatric ED in the new hospital (a first in the region) that will complement expanded pediatric services in the existing facility.

2014

Giant air conditioning units are lifted into building that will eventually manage and clean the massive amounts of air that circulate through the new hospital.

Now

The doors of the new hospital officially opened to the public on September 26th and a ribbon-cutting ceremony was held outside of the new Emergency Department to celebrate the milestone.
